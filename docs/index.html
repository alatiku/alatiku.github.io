About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Aliyu Atiku Mustapha, and I’m a hard-working professional who is now pursuing a career in the fascinating world of data science.

My interest in data science was sparked working in the commodity import/export industry, where I saw firsthand how important data is to comprehending market trends, patterns, and pricing. This inspired me to go even more deeply into the world of data analysis.

I eagerly enrolled in the esteemed Google Data Analysis course, honing my skills in data exploration and extracting valuable insights. Hungry for more knowledge, I pursued additional classes specializing in leveraging the power of R for data analysis. As my proficiency in R grew, I found myself increasingly captivated by the intersection of data science and machine learning. Motivated by this new found interest, I am currently expanding my expertise in python, with emphasis on machine learning.

As I embark on this career transition, my main objective is to apply my growing skills to data from the real world. I am motivated to use data science tools to uncover insightful information, identify trends, and solve complex problems. I’ve actively contributed to the Bioconductor project by converting the “graph” and “OrganismDbi” vignettes into HTML versions, enhancing accessibility and usability.

I invite you to explore my personal portfolio projects. All codes are hidden by default, but can be displayed by clicking the ‘Code’ button at the top-right corner of each page and selecting ‘Show All Code’ or by clicking individual “Show” buttons along the right-side of the page.

Thank you for visiting!
