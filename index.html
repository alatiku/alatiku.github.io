About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm a data-driven Business Operations Manager turned Data Scientist.

My interest in data science was sparked working in the commodity import/export industry, where I saw first hand how important data is to comprehending market trends, patterns, and pricing. This inspired me to go even more deeply into the world of data by taking the Google Data Analytics course, later enrolling into SQL, R and Python data science specialization courses.

My main objective is to apply my growing skills in data manipulation (wrangling and cleaning), analysis, and visualization, hypothesis testing, A/B testing, and machine learning algorithms to uncover insightful information, identify trends, and solve complex business problems.
